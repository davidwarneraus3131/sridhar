Agents, Admin & Customers

Newadobes is a role-based real estate web platform that connects customers, property agents, and admins in a single system. The goal is to make it easy for users to discover nearby properties and contact agents without any friction.

Customer Experience

Customers can browse and search properties with filters such as location, budget, and property type. They can quickly view details, check nearby options, and explore properties with a clean, mobile-friendly interface. From the property page, they can easily contact the agent for more information or a site visit.

Agent Dashboard

Agents get a dedicated dashboard to manage their listings, update property details, track inquiries, and respond to customer leads. They can publish, edit, and control the visibility of their properties, making it simple to keep their portfolio up to date.

Admin Controls

Admins can oversee the entire platform with role-based access control. They can manage users (agents and customers), review and approve property listings, and monitor overall activity to keep the platform safe, clean, and reliable.

Key Features

  • Customer, Agent, and Admin role-based access and dashboards.
  • Property listing with filters and easy nearby location discovery.
  • Direct contact flow between customers and agents.
  • Mobile-responsive design for a smooth experience on all devices.

This project showcases a complete real estate workflow with clear roles, user-friendly navigation, and practical features for real-world usage.
