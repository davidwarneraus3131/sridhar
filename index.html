How It Works?

  • Customers browse and order food items through the web app.
  • Orders are added to the cart for review.
  • Payment is securely processed via **Razorpay integration**.
  • After successful payment, the order is **confirmed automatically**.
  • Cashier scans the **QR Code** to verify the order details.
  • Once verified, the customer can collect their food.

Admin Features

The system comes with an advanced **admin panel** where administrators can:

  • Manage **users** and their orders.
  • Add, edit, and delete **food items**.
  • Track **order history** and payment records.
  • Monitor **stock availability** in real-time.
  • Generate **sales reports** and insights.
